Description

The Low Top 04 is a modern interpretation of 90’s tennis sneakers. We’ve edited the design down to enhance its most iconic features, without losing that retro vibe. The stitching and colours are a direct reinterpretation of a vintage tennis court. It features an upper made from stitched nappa leather, a padded ankle collar and tongue, and nylon laces. The natural rubber outsoles enhance that old-school feel even more.

  • BIOCOURO - Metal free tanned leather
  • Handmade in Portugal
  • onSteam Bio-eco lining, sustainable polyester and corn blend
  • ETQ design - Memory foam insoles with moisture management
  • Extremely flexible Strobel constructed soles
  • Tonal metal eyelets and nylon laces
  • TPU outsole
  • Comes in a branded box with multifunctional dustbags
