A luxurious take on classic tennis sneakers.

We feel safe to say that the tennis sneaker is today's most essential footwear silhouette. It's both chic and relaxed, it bridges all seasons and it literally lightens every outfit.

Description

Our signature styles never change, but constantly improve: the LT 01 was
launched in 2010 as a luxurious interpretation of classic tennis sneakers
and is easily our most recognizable style. This time around, our same-
but-better Essence Series features this iconic silhouette in five timeless
colorways. Our crisp white colorway is a real wardrobe essential. We’ve
used the best version of leather available: no metal chemicals are used in
processing the hide, increasing comfort while reducing your eco-
footprint.

  • BIOCOURO - Metal-free tanned leather
  • Handmade in Portugal
  • onSteam® Microfibre lining
  • ETQ design - Memory foam insoles with moisture management
  • Extremely flexible Strobel constructed soles
  • Natural rubber outsoles - blooming may occur
  • Comes in a branded box with multifunctional dustbags
  • Replenishment service - free laces
