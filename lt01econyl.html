Description

When it comes to innovation, we like to experiment with unconventional materials that offer circular design solutions. We believe closing the loop is the future and using ECONYL® is part of our sustainable journey. This synthetic yarn is made from repurposed fishing nets and ocean waste. It offers endless possibilities for tech-driven footwear that’s made to last. The beauty of regenerated nylon is that it can be recycled infinitely, without ever losing its quality or choking up the oceans. Our knitted uppers are lightweight, durable, and fused seamlessly onto our reinforced TPU outsoles.

  • ECONYL® - Regenerated nylon.
  • Hand made in Portugal.
  • Antibacterial microfibre lining.
  • ETQ design - Memory foam insoles with moisture management.
  • Reinforced TPU outsole.
  • Comes in branded box with multifunctional dustbags.
  • Replenishment service - free laces.
