ETQ Laundry Service
ETQ Laundry Service

Standing by our product’s quality and
wanting to help you extend its lifespan, we
are happy to offer you ETQ Laundry: a
cleaning service in our Amsterdam flagship store.

How does it work?

Step 1 – Pick-up / Drop-off
For now, we offer this service in
Amsterdam only (certain areas excluded).
We will pick-up your shoes at home or you
come by our Flagship store, situated at
Singel 465, and drop off your worn pair.
Later on, we will collect pairs through
postal service from customers outside
Amsterdam.

Step 2 – Cleaning
We will apply a full sneaker service
routine, using ETQ x SneakerLAB®*
products to clean your ETQ’s. Please note
this might take up to 5 business days.

Step 3 – Pick-up or delivery
Once we are done, we will reach out to
arrange a pick-up time or delivery
moment.

Pricing

Cleaning & new laces: €20*
Home pick-up & delivery: €5

*The €20 that is paid now will be
deducted off your next pair. How? For the
amount that is charged you will receive a
coupon to use on our webshop. This
coupon is valid for one year.

Drop-off your worn shoes or schedule a home-pick-up:

This service is only available in Amsterdam
(some areas excluded). We will pick-up
your shoes by bike, take them back to the
store and fully service them. Just click on
the widget at the bottom right of your
screen and say hello to schedule a pick-up
appointment. Once we are done, you will
receive an e-mail to schedule a delivery
moment.

Natural shoe care solutions

We have developed a harmless and
effective range of care products in
collaboration with SneakerLAB®: a South-
African supplier of natural shoe care
formulas.
